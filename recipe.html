Delicious Momos

Ingredients

For the Dough

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup water (approx., enough to knead a soft dough)
  • 1 teaspoon oil (optional, for smoothness)

For the Filling(veg)

  • 2 cups finely chopped cabbage
  • 1 cup finely chopped onion
  • ½ cup grated carrot
  • 2–3 finely chopped spring onions
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 2 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Prepare the filling
  3. Shape the momo
  4. Steam the momo
Enjoy your delicious momos
